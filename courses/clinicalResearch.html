Clinical Research Fundamentals #Research⮟

Learn the basics of clinical research, including study design, trial phases, protocols, regulatory guidelines, and clinical data structures essential for any clinical research role.

Data Collection & Validation #DataPrep⮟

Master techniques for collecting, cleaning, and validating clinical trial and patient data to ensure accuracy and compliance with regulatory standards.

Clinical Data Management & Automation #Automation⮟

Automate data workflows, generate reports, and manage clinical datasets using SAS and other clinical data management tools.

Statistical Analysis in Clinical Research #Statistics⮟

Perform statistical analyses including descriptive statistics, ANOVA, regression, survival analysis, and other methods relevant to clinical studies.

Data Visualization & Reporting #Visualization⮟

Build tables, listings, and figures (TLFs), create visualizations, and generate clinical study reports (CSRs) compliant with CDISC and regulatory standards.

Advanced SAS & Clinical Programming #Programming⮟

Learn advanced SAS programming for clinical data, including macros, SQL in SAS, arrays, loops, and custom solutions for trial datasets.

Clinical Databases & Integration #Databases⮟

Manage and integrate clinical trial datasets, CDISC SDTM/ADaM standards, and query data from relational databases for analysis.

External Data & EDC Integration #Integration⮟

Access and integrate clinical data from electronic data capture systems (EDCs), APIs, and other external sources for comprehensive analysis.

Capstone Project #Project⮟

Work on a real-world clinical research project: manage and validate trial datasets, perform statistical analyses, generate TLFs, and prepare regulatory-compliant clinical study reports.
